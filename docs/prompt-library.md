## How to Write a Good AI Prompt (Product Photography)

### Core principles
- **Clarity**: Be explicit about the setting, mood, lighting, and textures.
- **Descriptiveness**: Include palette, materials, and atmosphere, but avoid over-constraining.
- **Creativity**: Add unique contextual elements that match your brand.
- **Open-endedness**: Leave room for the model to interpret and enhance.

### Product photography prompt checklist
- **Product context**: Identify the product and its audience.
- **Style**: Match the brand aesthetic (minimal, luxury, playful, rustic).
- **Contextual elements**: Include environment cues that support the product story.
- **Texture and lighting**: Specify background textures and lighting quality.
- **Brand alignment**: Reinforce brand colors and values.
